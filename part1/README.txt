This data package includes the training and validation datasets for the AI CUP 2023 competition "Teaching Computers to Watch Badminton Matches". The dataset is comprised of rally videos that have been cropped from 15 BWF match videos and 3 self-recorded match videos. Each rally video provides a complete recording of a rally, from the serving to the dead bird. The videos are encoded in mp4 format, while the shot-by-shot data files are in .csv format. The videos have a frame rate of 30FPS, a resolution of 1280x720, and a bitrate of 1500kbps.
